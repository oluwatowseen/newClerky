Public Benefit Corporations

Form your startup as a Delaware public benefit corporation, for added flexibility to make your startup a force for good. Clerky products help you form your PBC startup correctly, so that you can go on to raise money and hire people without issue.

What's the difference?

Regular Delaware corporation are obligated to maximize stockholder value.

Delaware public benefit corporations must balance stockholder value with one or more specified public benefits, as well as the interests of anyone materially affected by its conduct.
